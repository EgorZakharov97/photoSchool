How does it work¿

It's easy! Each of our studying programs is made of two parts. Online and offline. When you register for a workshop you get access to a student portal with video tutorials, presets, and the most important - workshop group chats, where you can chat with other students and your coaches.

Each workshop is made of several parts.

Theory - this is where we get to know each other and talk about theoretical background based on the workshop theme.

Practice - this is where it's getting interesting. We set up a whole set for you with a model, makeup artists, stylist, and unique concept. We take pictures together, then you take pictures by yourself!

Feedback - after the workshop you will have some photos to work with. We will ask you to send us your top 3 images, so we can give you some feedback on the composition, color, light, and other aspects.

Dive into the theory

We will explain to you the most complicated concept of photography using the most understandable methods. We know how boring the theoretical part of education can be, that's why we divided all the information into offline and online blocks.

You will have video tutorials, posing guides, and reading recommendations. At the same time, we will provide you with a strong base during our in-person workshops, passing the knowledge we gained from world-class artists.

Watch courses and vebinars

You will have acess to video tutorials, lessons and vebinars with other artists and students. This acess is exclusive and limited to 1 month after your workshop date.

Study when practice.

Yes! You are going to take photos.

During our workshops, each student is going to have some time to take pictures one on one with a model at the set that we've prepared for you.

Build a unique portfolio.

Each of our workshops is going to have a unique concept behind it. It means that the photos you take during the workshop are going to be "out of this world".

Learn to work with a team.

We will have a serious team on each workshop set. This is your chance to learn how to interact with other photographers, models, and stylists.
