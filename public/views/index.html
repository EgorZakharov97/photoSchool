How does it work?

First,you have to find and choose a workshop that you are interested in.

When you register for a workshop you get access to a student portal with video tutorials, presets, and the most important - workshop group chats, where youcan chat with other students and your coaches.

EACH WORKSHOP IS MADE OF THREE PARTS:

Theory - this is where we get to know each other and talk about theoretical background based on the workshop theme.

Practice - this is where it's getting interesting. We set up a whole set foryou with a model, makeup artists, stylist, and unique concept. We take picturestogether, then you take pictures by yourself!

Feedback - after the workshop you will have some photos to work with. We willask you to send us your top 3 images, so we can give you some feedback on thecomposition, color, light, and other aspects.

Who is it for?

For Beginners

Get ready. It's time to switch into a beast mode. We will help you discover that you can shoot better. Way better. We will help you to build your portfolio and gain creativity.

For Professionals

We are not here to show you some basics!
We focus on your creativity first and there's always a challenging "out of the box" concept explained in each workshop. Get ready for a challenge!

Not Protographers

Looking for a new hobby? Bored? We gotch you. Explore photography and learn new skills with us. Explore the world through an artistic approach.
It's fun!

Dive into the theory

We will explain to you the most complicated concept of photography using the most understandable methods. We know how boring the theoretical part of education can be, that's why we divided all the information into offline and online blocks.

You will have video tutorials, posing guides, and reading recommendations. At the same time, we will provide you with a strong base during our in-person workshops, passing the knowledge we gained from world-class artists.

Watch courses and vebinars

You will have access to video tutorials, lessons and vebinars with other artists and students. This access is exclusive and limited to 1 month after your workshop date.

Study and practice.

Yes! You are going to take photos.

During our workshops, each student is going to have some time to take pictures one on one with a model at the set that we've prepared for you.

Build a unique portfolio.

Each of our workshops is going to have a unique concept behind it. It means that the photos you take during the workshop are going to be "out of this world".

Learn to work with a team.

We will have a serious team on each workshop set. This is your chance to learn how to interact with other photographers, models, and stylists.
